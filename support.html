Quick answers

Restore Subscriptions Billing Privacy

Restore Purchases

Open the app → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID you used to subscribe.

Manage or Cancel Subscription

On iPhone: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions → Ottey
Or visit apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Paid but still on Free

Tap Restore Purchases in Settings. If it still shows Free, email us with your Apple receipt (no card info).

Purchase won’t complete

  • Check App Store sign-in and payment method.
  • Reboot the device and try again.
  • If you’re testing, ensure Sandbox is signed in (Developer settings).

Contact

Email: support@ottey.ai

Include device model, iOS version, app version (Settings → About), and screenshots if possible.
