Restore Purchases

Open Ottey → Settings → Restore Purchases. Make sure you’re signed into the same Apple ID used to buy Ottey+.

Manage or Cancel Subscription

On iPhone: Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Ottey.
Or open apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions.

Paid but still on Free

Tap Settings → Restore Purchases. If it still shows Free, email us with your Apple receipt (no card info).

Purchase won’t complete

  • Check App Store sign-in and payment method.
  • Reboot device and try again.
  • Email logs/time of attempt to support@ottey.ai.
